Process Over Product

Admittedly, I found it difficult not to get caught up in trying to make my site look good. However, for the sake of getting things done there are a few things I've improvised in order just to get things done, though I know I haven't come to a solution via a technically correct method. My left navigation column is a prime example of this. In order to make this display without a massive gap at the bottom of the menu nav I've set the bottom padding to 200%. I'm completely sure it's not the right thing to do, but it will do for now. I'd also like to change to container width, but at the moment I'm having trouble doing that without throwing everything out. So, there are certainly a few things on the to-do list.
