C7 Motivation

'pleasure, passion and purpose'

Thinking and learning more about happiness and motivation helped lead me to E.D.A. I think that it is very easy to lose both passion and pleasure if you don't feel a sense of purpose. I'm not sure at the moment that I have a strong passion for coding, but I do feel reward in being deeply engaged in tasks and being able to achieve a sense of flow. The greater motivator for me is that I will be able to use my coding skills in meaningful ways and work with others who have similar values and goals.

Core Values:

  • Kindness
  • Respect
  • Curiosity
  • Creativity
  • Balance
  • Excellence

I stand for:

equality, fairness and justice. I respect and value difference and seek to work with others for a common good.

Resilience, meditation and other musings:

I meditated on two very similar situations; a public talk that went very well, and one that did not. Mindfulness readings and exercises have helped me to reflect on such situations in a more constructive and objective way. As a result I feel I'm able to look back on a situation such as the talk that did not go well and analyse what went wrong and how I could avoid repeating my mistakes, rather than dwelling on the emotions related to failure.
